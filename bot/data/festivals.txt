### SECTION: ASHWAMEDHA_OVERVIEW ###
Ashwamedha is the flagship technical fest of Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering and Technology (ASIET), celebrated for its grandeur and excellence. As a national-level technical fiesta, Ashwamedha attracts some of the brightest technical minds from across the country, providing a competitive platform where innovation, creativity, and technical prowess are put to the test.

### SECTION: ASHWAMEDHA_COMPETITIONS ###
Ashwamedha technical competitions and events: The two-day technical festival is packed with a variety of technical competitions held simultaneously across the campus. These contests challenge participants to solve complex problems, innovate solutions, and demonstrate their technical skills. Events typically include coding challenges, robotics competitions, hackathons, and various domain-specific contests such as circuit design, mechanical design, and software development.

### SECTION: ASHWAMEDHA_FEATURES ###
Ashwamedha fest features: Students from different parts of the nation converge at ASIET to take part in Ashwamedha. Professional shows are organized on both days providing entertainment. The fest serves as a networking platform where students interact with industry professionals, alumni, and peers, opening doors for future collaborations, internships, and job opportunities. The festival helps students enhance their technical knowledge and skills through workshops and seminars conducted by experts.
